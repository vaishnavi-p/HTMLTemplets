Available online courses

1. DataCamp

Datacamp is a very good platform for those who want to start from the scratch. It focuses more on the coding part from the 1st chapter itself.

Click here to open the course at DataCamp

2. Learnpython.org

Learnpython.org is similar to datacamp and it is also free of cost. You can choose any one of the two if you are a beginner.

Click here to open the course at learnpython.org

3. EdX

If you like to learn from video lectures. This place is just for you. Apart from videos, it also contains labs at the end of each module for the hands-on practice of codes.

Important: - You don’t have to pay if you want to learn this course. You can audit the course and start learning. edX doesn’t provide certificates for the audit courses.
